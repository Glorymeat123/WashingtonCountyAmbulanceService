Mission Statement

Our mission at Washington County Ambulance Service is to provide the highest level of emergency medical care and transportation services to our community. We are dedicated to serving with compassion, professionalism, and integrity.

Training Department Mission Statement

Our Training Department is committed to providing high-quality education and training to ensure our team is equipped with the latest knowledge and skills in emergency medical care. We strive to foster a culture of continuous learning and improvement.

State-of-the-Art Equipment and Protocols

At Washington County Ambulance Service, we utilize state-of-the-art equipment and advanced protocols to ensure the best possible care for our patients. Our ambulances are equipped with the latest technology, and our team follows stringent protocols to provide efficient and effective medical care.
